Digital India

Digital India is a campaign launched by the Government of India in order to ensure the Government's services are made available to citizens electronically by improved online infrastructure and by increasing Internet connectivity or making the country digitally empowered in the field of technology.

Is Digital India successful?

This campaign has made the country digitally empowered in the area of technology. The regulated government of India has made its grounds for making the availability of government services electronically. The enhanced online infrastructure and by extending Internet connectivity have eased the work of citizens.
